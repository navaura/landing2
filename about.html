Our Story

Founded in 2023 and incorporated in 2025 and headquartered in Kolkata, Navaura Arctiq (OPC) Private Limited was born out of a bold vision: to empower businesses of all sizes with cutting-edge AI, automation, and scalable software solutions—without the usual cost and complexity.

Our founder, Subham Mukherjee, a seasoned technologist and entrepreneur, launched Navaura Arctiq to solve deep-rooted inefficiencies in software deployment, customer engagement, and AI integration. The company reflects the ethos "Sarvesham Krute Bhabishyam"—The Future for All.

Today, we are rapidly growing as a trusted AI partner for startups, SMEs, and enterprises across industries—delivering tools like Vedi AI, SAYVA, and bespoke automation systems that redefine what's possible with software.
